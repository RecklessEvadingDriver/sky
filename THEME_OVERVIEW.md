## 🚀 How to Use This Theme

### Quick Start (3 Steps)

1. **Upload to Shopify**
   - Go to Online Store → Themes → Add theme
   - Upload this theme folder as a ZIP
   - Publish the theme

2. **Configure Settings**
   - Go to Themes → Customize
   - Update WhatsApp number (Theme Settings)
   - Add your logo and contact information
   - Customize colors and content

3. **Add Tours**
   - Create collections: domestic-tours, international-tours, honeymoon-packages
   - Add products (tours) to collections
   - Fill in tour metafields (duration, inclusions, etc.)
   - Assign product template to `product.skystar`

**Detailed instructions**: See `INSTALLATION.md`

## 🎯 Business Model

This theme is designed for the **inquiry-based booking model**:

1. Customer browses tour packages on your website
2. Customer clicks "Inquire on WhatsApp" button
3. Conversation happens on WhatsApp
4. Booking confirmed via WhatsApp/email/phone
5. Payment collected offline or via WhatsApp Pay/UPI

**Why this model?**
- More personal customer interaction
- No payment gateway fees
- Flexibility in pricing and customization
- Common in travel industry
- Builds trust through conversation

## 🔄 Need E-commerce Features?

The theme currently hides cart/checkout to focus on WhatsApp lead generation. To re-enable e-commerce:

1. Edit `assets/skystar-custom.css`
2. Search for `/* Hide Cart/Checkout */`
3. Remove or comment out the hiding rules
4. Enable Shopify Payments in your store settings
